About Us

The Entrepreneurship & Business Club (EBC) at PP Savani University is dedicated to fostering innovation, leadership, and business acumen among students. Our goal is to inspire and equip future entrepreneurs with the skills, knowledge, and network needed to turn ideas into successful ventures.


Our goal is to foster a community of initiative and motivated entrepreneurs who can support each other and drive economic growth in their region. We strive to create an environment where members can share their experience, knowledge, and resources, in order to help each other achieve their business goals

VISION

The vision of EBC CLUB is to foster a community of passionate and driven individuals who are dedicated to the success of their own business and the success of business around them.
