About Our Club

Empowering innovation, leadership, and entrepreneurship at PPSU.

Our Mission

To foster entrepreneurial spirit, cultivate leadership, and support students in building innovative business ideas with real-world impact.

Our Vision

To create a thriving ecosystem where students collaborate, learn, and launch ventures that shape a better future.

Our Story

Founded in 2022, the PPSU EBC Club started as a small initiative to connect students passionate about startups and leadership. Over the years, it has grown into a community of changemakers, innovators, and aspiring business leaders, hosting dozens of events, workshops, and speaker sessions.

Be a part of a growing movement of student leaders, thinkers, and doers. Help us drive change through innovation and entrepreneurship.
